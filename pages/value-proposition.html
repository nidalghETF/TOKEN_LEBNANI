Value Proposition

Core Value Proposition

Token Lebnani solves the critical liquidity problem for Lebanese real estate by creating a regulated bridge between illiquid physical assets and the digital investment expectations of GCC investors.

Unlocking $150B in Trapped Wealth

The Lebanese diaspora holds an estimated $150 billion in wealth, much of it sitting in low-yield accounts due to the inability to safely invest back home. Token Lebnani provides the regulated pathway to unlock this value.

ADGM Regulatory Safety

By domiciling in the Abu Dhabi Global Market, we provide institutional-grade legal protection under English Common Law, addressing the trust gap that has prevented diaspora investment in Lebanese assets.

Non-Custodial Security Model

Unlike traditional platforms, we operate under "Option A" - investors retain full custody of their assets via their own wallets, eliminating counterparty risk while maintaining regulatory compliance.

Market Differentiation

Asset-First Approach

While competitors often sell tokens first and seek assets later (creating synthetic risk), Token Lebnani acquires and legally structures the physical asset before tokenization begins. This ensures each token represents genuine ownership in a tangible asset with verified title.

Capital-Efficient Regulatory Strategy

By utilizing the ADGM "Category 4: Arranging" license framework and partnering with existing MTFs for secondary liquidity, we avoid the $4M+ capital requirements of custody licenses and $140,000+ costs of exchange licenses, allowing us to operate lean while maintaining full compliance.

Immediate Liquidity Guarantee

Unlike traditional real estate investments with 5-10 year lockups, or even competitors with 12-24 month restrictions, Token Lebnani tokens can be traded on partner ADGM-licensed MTFs from day one, providing unprecedented liquidity for real estate investments.

Platform Economics

Revenue Model

Token Lebnani operates on a dual-revenue model:

  • Primary Issuance Fees: 3-5% of capital raised during initial token offerings
  • Asset Management Fees: 1-2% of Net Operating Income (NOI) collected annually

Cost Structure Advantage

Our "Net Zero Cost Model" for asset onboarding means initial setup costs are fully recoverable from transaction fees, requiring no long-term debt financing. This capital efficiency allows us to launch with minimal burn rate while proving the model in the ADGM Sandbox.
