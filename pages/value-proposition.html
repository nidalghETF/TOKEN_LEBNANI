THE 3-STEP PROCESS

The 3-Step Fractionalization Process

The mechanics are straightforward. We turn a brick-and-mortar asset into a liquid digital security.

  1. Acquisition: A property is acquired by a dedicated Lebanese Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV). This isolates risk.
  2. Tokenization: The ADGM Holding Company issues digital tokens representing the equity of that SPV. You are buying the legal right to the income generated by that building.
  3. Distribution: Investors purchase tokens via a secure dashboard, receiving legal rights to the property's income and capital appreciation.
