  • Activity: GCC Hunter networks with gatekeepers (lawyers, accountants, REA/IWA principals).
  • Output: Soft commitments (LOIs) + Pre-vetted REA/IWA partners.
  • Key Metric: Waitlist of qualified leads with high-intent status.

Phase 2: Private Viewing (Months 4–6)

  • Format: Exclusive 3–4 person dinners in Riyadh/Dubai/Beirut.
  • Collateral:
    • Physical "Ammo": High-end brochures/videos per asset (Lebanon Asset Originator).
    • Trust Anchors: ADGM license + YazConsult legal opinion (displayed prominently).
  • Pitch Script: "Own prime Lebanese real estate with institutional safety. ADGM regulates your title deed — not blockchain. Minimum entry: $25,000. Allocations limited."

Phase 3: White Glove Handoff (Months 6–9)

  • Workflow:
    1. Verbal "Yes"
    2. Client Services Coordinator takes over via WhatsApp
    3. Guides through 4 steps: KYC → Wallet Setup → Fiat-to-USDT → TPA Signing
    4. Post-onboarding relationship farming
  • Critical Rule: 0% partner involvement post-handoff. Coordinator resolves all tech friction live.
