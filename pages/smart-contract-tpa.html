Smart Contract & TPA Architecture

Executive Summary: Token Lebnani employs a dual-contract system to bridge digital ownership with legal enforceability[cite: 5]. The Smart Contract handles technical execution, while the Token Purchase Agreement (TPA) provides the legal framework under ADGM law[cite: 6].

1. The Dual-Contract Architecture

Digital records alone are insufficient for cross-border asset enforcement[cite: 11]. Our system relies on two synchronized pillars:

1. Smart Contract (Technical)

Role: Programmable equity mechanism[cite: 31].

  • Records ownership on immutable Ethereum ledger[cite: 32].
  • Automates compliance checks (Whitelisting)[cite: 33].
  • Maintains the definitive shareholder register[cite: 35].
  • Standard: ERC-1400 Security Token[cite: 37].

2. The TPA (Legal)

Role: Legal enforceability & protection[cite: 17].

  • Satisfies ADGM Category 4 regulations[cite: 20].
  • Defines enforceable rights under English Common Law[cite: 83].
  • Creates "Look-Through" mechanism to Lebanese assets[cite: 69].

2. Technical Specifications

The Smart Contract operates on the Ethereum mainnet using institutional-grade infrastructure[cite: 38].

  • Non-Custodial: Token Lebnani never holds investor private keys[cite: 49].
  • Whitelisting: Only KYC/AML-approved wallets can hold tokens[cite: 55].
  • Blacklist & Replace: If a wallet is compromised, a multi-sig protocol freezes the old tokens and mints replacements to the verified owner, ensuring assets are never permanently lost[cite: 50, 51, 52, 53].

3. Legal Enforcement (The TPA)

The TPA bridges the gap between blockchain records and real-world courts[cite: 86].

  • Arbitration: Binding Arbitration under the New York Convention (1958) ensures ADGM judgments are enforceable against Lebanese assets[cite: 84, 89].
  • Share Pledges: The ADGM Holding Company pledges shares of the Lebanese SPV as security[cite: 87].
  • Conflict Resolution: The TPA explicitly references the Smart Contract hash. If technology fails, the TPA provides the legal fallback[cite: 26, 28].

4. Integration Workflow

The operational flow for a new investor:

  • Pre-Investment: Investor completes KYC/AML via Waystone; wallet is whitelisted[cite: 100, 101].
  • Execution: Investor signs the TPA and wires funds to the regulated Escrow Agent[cite: 104, 105].
  • Token Delivery: Upon Escrow confirmation, the Smart Contract mints/transfers tokens to the investor[cite: 106].
  • Lifecycle: Dividends are settled off-chain (via bank transfer) but recorded on-chain for transparency[cite: 111].

5. Risk Mitigation

The system is designed to handle worst-case scenarios[cite: 178].

  • Funds Safety: Funds flow via regulated Escrow and never touch Token Lebnani accounts[cite: 187].
  • Liability: Ring-fenced SPVs contain liability within individual property entities[cite: 185].
  • Technical Audit: Formal security review by a tier-1 blockchain security firm[cite: 180].
