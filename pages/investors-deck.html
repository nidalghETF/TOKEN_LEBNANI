THE PRODUCT

3-Step Fractionalization Process

"The mechanics are straightforward. We identify a high-yield asset. We place it into a single-purpose entity to isolate risk. We then issue tokens that represent shares in that entity. When you buy a token, you aren't just buying a digital file; you are buying the legal right to the income generated by that building. This process turns a brick-and-mortar asset into a liquid digital security."

  1. Acquisition: A property is acquired by a dedicated Lebanese Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV).
  2. Tokenization: The ADGM Holding Company issues digital tokens representing the equity of that SPV.
  3. Distribution: Investors purchase tokens via a secure dashboard, receiving legal rights to the property's income and capital appreciation.
